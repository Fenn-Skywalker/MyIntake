Objective:
Collect all keys in each level to unlock the door 
After the door is unlocked reach it to 

Controls:
Left arrow key - Move left
Right arrow key - Move right
Up arrow key - Jump

Notes:
The player moves based on acceleration and velocity and will only automatically slow down when on the floor
and the player presses no keys, otherwise the player will "slide" in the direction of the last key pressed until his velocity reaches 0
so be mindful not to slip off edges!
